About Me

I’m a frontend/game developer with a passion for 3D.

Hey there! I'm Haleem, a self-taught creative from Lagos, Nigeria. I'm just beginning my frontend development journey, but my passion for building amazing things goes way back.

What got me into this? Anime, games, and the cool 3D worlds that always left me in awe. I’ve always wanted to create my own characters, stories, and experiences — stuff that makes people feel something.

Right now, I’m learning JavaScript and exploring Vue, Unity, and Blender. It’s a lot — but I love the challenge. My dream? To model, rig, and animate a fully original 3D character in Blender, use it in Unity, and share the journey on a website I build myself.

When I’m not coding or learning, I’m gaming (Genshin, Honkai: Star Rail, ZZZ, Wuthering Waves), shooting hoops, or diving into manga worlds.

This is just the start for me — and I’m excited to keep learning and creating.
